## ROI & Investment Data

- **Smart building ROI:** ~3:1 over 5 years (JLL estimate)
- **AI-driven HVAC payback:** 2–5 years
- **LEED certification rental premium:** 8.5–25%
- **85%** of CRE decision-makers plan to increase tech investments (JLL, 2023)
- **92%** of occupiers believe IoT solutions provide competitive advantage
- **91%** of tenants willing to pay premium for tech-enhanced workspaces
- **75%** of CRE/retail executives consider smart buildings essential (Forrester/Johnson Controls, 2024)

## Cautionary Data

- **IoT project success rate:** Only 16% fully realized (Beecham Research, 2023; up from 12% in 2020)
- **Some hesitancy emerging:** Unmet ROI expectations, exaggerated vendor claims, ongoing SaaS cost concerns
- **66%** report positive ROI from building management technologies (Forbes/Intel survey)

## Regulatory Drivers

- **EU EPBD mandate:** From 2025, non-residential buildings with heating >290 kW must install interoperable BACS; threshold drops to 70 kW by 2029
- **US IRA funding:** $3.4B for federal building portfolio; $1B specifically for smart/sustainable technologies; $80M GSA initiative for smart building tech across 560 federal buildings
- **GSA peak load reduction potential:** 165 MW across federal portfolio through GEB strategies

## Cybersecurity

- 85 notable vendors identified across IT, OT, and consulting backgrounds
- IT/OT convergence creating expanded attack surface
- Priority difference: IT (Confidentiality-Integrity-Availability) vs. OT (Availability-Integrity-Confidentiality)
- Standards: NIST 800-53, IEC 62443 (parts 4-1, 4-2, 3-3), ISO 27001, FIPS 140-3
